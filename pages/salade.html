Easiest salade Recipe

Ingredients

  • Lettuce
  • Cucumber
  • Salade dressing
  • Salade seasoning
  • Litteraly anything else

Preparation

  1. Take the lettuce leaves and cut them
  2. Put them in a salade bowl
  3. Put all the ingredients in the bowl
  4. Gently mix everything
  5. Boom! Salade!
