Best Nachos Recipe

Ingredients

  • A bag of good tortillas chips
  • A lot of shredded nachos cheese
  • Some sliced black olives
  • Banana peppers to add some heat
  • Salsa
  • Guac
  • Sourcream

Preparation

  1. Put the chips on a plate
  2. Put the cheese on the chips
  3. Put all of the Ingredients, but the sauce, on the cheese
  4. 375 degres F for 13 minutes
  5. Enjoy!
